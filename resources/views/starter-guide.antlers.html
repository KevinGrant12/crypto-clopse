Crypto Staking

Now that you are up and running with your favorite exchange and trusty new wallet, let's explore how you can "stake" your cryptocurrencies and start earning more everyday. Staking simply means locking up your cryptocurrency in a liquidity pool to provide continuity to the network. When you stake your coins, you will earn a percentage of the crypto you have locked up. Think of it like dividends, but your returns come at a much faster rate.

Staking can be done on wallets AND exchanges. Not all coins can be staked and each stakeable coin varies in percentage and reward. Checkout the options below to start staking now:
